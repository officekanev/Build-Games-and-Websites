Slow Goods takes its core direction from various elements of the overall slow movement and applying it to the design and manufacturing of goods. It focuses on the usage of craftspeople within the manufacturing, sustainable material and slow production runs. Each piece has a story and PK Interior Design produces the products in cooperation with small local manufacturers and finally avoides the shipment across the globe. Last but not least Quality, Design and Functionality have to be assured.
